What is this?

This is a shared documentation site built and maintained by two computer science students at Birzeit University. It started as a place to turn lecture slides into readable, searchable notes; and grew into something more.

Everything here is written by hand, converted from real course material, and organized to actually make sense.

Courses

The courses/ section covers every subject we encounter throughout college, whatever gets thrown at us. Notes are organized by chapter and written to be actually worth reading.

If you are a student at BZU and want to contribute, you are welcome. Open a pull request, add your notes under courses/, and follow the existing structure. The more people contribute, the more useful this becomes for everyone.

Contribute at github.com/altaweelmustafa/docs

Books

The books/ section may get philosophical. When we read something worth talking about: technical, scientific, or otherwise, we write about it here. Not just summaries, but analysis, disagreements, things that stuck, and things that did not. Think of it less as a book report and more as thinking out loud in public.

APIs

Any API built by us gets its documentation here. Structured, versioned, and written to actually help someone use it. If you are trying to integrate with something and landed here, you are in the right place.
